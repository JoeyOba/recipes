Jollof-Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and a variety of spices. While there are many regional variations, here is a basic list of ingredients for making Jollof rice:

Ingredients

  • Rice: 2 cups (long-grain parboiled rice is commonly used)
  • Tomatoes: 4-5 medium-sized tomatoes or 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • Tomato paste: 2-3 tablespoons
  • Onions: 1-2 medium-sized onions (diced)
  • Red bell pepper: 1-2, diced
  • Scotch bonnet pepper or hot pepper: 1-2 (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Vegetable oil or palm oil: 1/4 to 1/2 cup (palm oil is traditional but vegetable oil is commonly used)
  • Chicken or vegetable broth: 2-3 cups
  • Bay leaves: 2-3
  • Thyme: 1 teaspoon (dried or fresh)
  • Curry powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Ground paprika or cayenne pepper: 1/2 teaspoon (optional, for additional heat)
  • Salt: to taste
